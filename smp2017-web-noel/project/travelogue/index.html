My travelogue

This summer I had been to Kerala and Bangalore. Kerala often referred to as God's own Country; blessed abundantly with serene natural beauty; from calm beaches to the mighty fog-laden hills, the surreal backwaters and plethora of ecological diversities, from the guileless elephants to the towering coconut trees. Kerala has many beautifull artistic churches and temples which will surely leave anyone spellbound. With such a majestic milieu you certainly are gonna feel closer to the God's heavenly abode. Bangalore, a bustling city with a lots of high-rise buildings, techno parks and traffic, mostly constituting a hell lot of the IT bunch running the Indian Silicon valley, but still in its heart Bangalore is an old soul also known as the Garden city with many lakes and gardens and most importantly having wonderful mild climate all-round the year. This travelogue is filled with my reminiscences of this trip.
